HOW WE BEGAN

MCB Clinical Research Centers, LLC (MCBCRC) is named after my grandmother, Marie Claire Bossaert. In 1985 she was diagnosed with colon cancer and given 3-6 months to live. She was told to “take care of your affairs” and the doctor walked out of the room.

After hearing this we researched the disease and found out about clinical trials available for her. She went on multiple studies and lived for 6 years. During this time she met three more grandchildren, learned how to ski and traveled to Europe. She passed away on February 17, 1991.

Since she passed away it has been our goal to ensure patients have access to clinical trials in lieu of “taking care of their affairs”.

Kristin Scalva -President

We promote quality of life for ourselves and others through the work that we do.
